Smart Savings Strategies

The power of automating your savings

Setting up an automatic transfer on payday removes the temptation to spend first and save what's left. Studies show automated savers save 40% more on average than those who manually transfer money. Set it, forget it, and watch your goal approach.

High-yield savings vs regular savings account

Traditional savings accounts at big banks often pay just 0.01%–0.1% APY. High-yield savings accounts at online banks currently offer 4%–5% APY — that's 40–500× more. On a $10,000 balance, that's the difference between $10 and $450 in annual interest, completely passively.
