Loan Repayment Strategies

How to reduce your total interest paid

Making even one extra payment per year — applied directly to principal — can cut years off your loan and save thousands in interest. For a 48-month $15,000 loan at 8.5%, paying just $50 extra monthly saves over $300 and eliminates 3 months of payments.

Fixed vs variable rate loans

Fixed rate loans keep the same interest rate for the life of the loan — predictable and safe if rates rise. Variable rate loans may start lower but can increase with market rates. Unless you plan to pay off quickly, fixed rates are safer for large, long-term loans.
